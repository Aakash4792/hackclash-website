About

HC Educates 2.0 is a 48-Hour Hackathon with a series of essential Technical Workshops. It is the perfect opportunity for you to learn and grow your skills in the field, and earn numerous certificates in an array of topics. There is only a one-time registration fee. Once you register for HC Educates 2.0, you get access to all workshops and a fair chance as a participant in the Hackathon.
